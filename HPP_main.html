Welcome to High Power Performance your home for muscule car and diesel truck upgrades at a reasonable price! Here, you ask for an estimate for engines, wheels, shocks, brakes, the works! Main mechanics are Ian Jansing-Kaestner and David Noe Jr.

Ian Jansing-Kaestner

Ian Jansing-Kaestner

Born in 1992 in Louisville, KY, Ian took an interest in fixing vehicles early in his teens. He rebuilt the engine for a Ford Ranger in 2010 as a capstone project for high school. He joined the Marines soon after and became a helicopter mechanic during his time in the Marines. He returned from the Marines and began working on cars. Currently, he is studying at the Nashville Auto Diesel College for diesel and high performance engines.

David Charles Noe Jr

David Charles Noe Jr
